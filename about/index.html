About Greyveil Editions

What is Greyveil?

Greyveil Editions is an independent publishing and research initiative exploring the patterns that shape human experience—identity, perception, emotion, behaviour, meaning, and the contradictions that exist within them.

Through books, essays, research projects, and long-form explorations, Greyveil seeks not only to understand how people think, but also how they live, change, struggle, and grow.

Its purpose is not simply to provide answers.

Its purpose is to encourage deeper observation, honest reflection, and a more conscious understanding of oneself and the world.

Greyveil is built on a simple belief:

Every life contains a story.
Every story contains a pattern.
And every pattern reveals something about what it means to be human.

Founder

LALIT R. MISHRA

Lalit R. Mishra is the founder of Greyveil Editions, an independent exploration of human nature and the inner life.

His work examines the patterns, emotions, perceptions, contradictions, and questions that shape the human experience. Drawing from psychology, philosophy, observation, and lived experience, he explores not only how people think, but why they become who they are.

Rather than presenting fixed answers, his writing is driven by curiosity—a continuous attempt to understand the relationship between mind, emotion, awareness, and action.

Greyveil Editions serves as the home for this ongoing exploration.

Because every life contains a story.
Every story contains a pattern.
And every pattern reveals something about what it means to be human.
