Summary

Answers can become new questions.

This book studies the human need to explain pain, uncertainty, silence, and contradiction.

It asks whether explanation resolves confusion or simply gives the mind a more elegant place to hide it.

Core Question

What if explanation is not the end of uncertainty, but another form of it?
